### 3. Weekly Learning Structure (Sample Framework)

This is a flexible template designed for a high-achieving student to maintain rigor while allowing for passion projects and well-being. Adjust hours based on credit load and course difficulty.

*   **Dedicated Academic Time (Approx. 25-30 hours/week, 3-4 hours per course):**
    *   **Lecture/Recitation Attendance:** Non-negotiable active participation.
    *   **Pre-Lecture Prep (1 hour per course):** Skim readings, review previous notes, identify potential questions. This significantly improves comprehension during lectures.
    *   **Post-Lecture Review & Consolidation (1-1.5 hours per course):** Re-read notes, summarize key points, work through example problems, identify areas for further study.
    *   **Problem-Solving/Project Work (1.5-2 hours per course):** Dedicated time for assignments, coding exercises, project development. Break large tasks into smaller, manageable chunks.
    *   **Weekly Deep Dive (2-3 hours):** Focus on one challenging concept from any course, or explore an advanced topic related to your AI/Sustainability goals beyond coursework.
*   **Structured Extracurricular & Personal Development (Approx. 10-15 hours/week):**
    *   **Student Projects/Hackathons (5-8 hours):** Dedicated blocks. Ensure these align with your goals and don't completely overshadow academics.
    *   **Volunteer Initiatives (2-3 hours):** As you value personal growth and teamwork.
    *   **Networking/Mentorship (1-2 hours):** Attend talks, connect with faculty or industry professionals.
    *   **Physical Activity/Hobbies (Minimum 5 hours):** Crucial for mental well-being and preventing burnout.
    *   **Social Time/Relaxation (Flexible):** Essential for a balanced life.
*   **Daily Rhythm:**
    *   **Morning:** Review goals for the day, tackle challenging academic tasks when fresh.
    *   **Afternoon:** Lectures, project work, study group sessions.
    *   **Evening:** Lighter academic review, planning, personal interests, relaxation.
    *   **Weekends:** Deeper dives into projects, catch-up, advanced reading, personal time.
*   **Regular Review:** At the end of each week, review your progress, identify what worked well, and adjust your schedule for the following week.
